Soda Wars: $3.99 for a Case of Coke

published on June 18, 2024
Last summer, the average price for a case of soda was $6.12 in grocery stores. Beverage manufacturers preferred it that way, before soda price wars broke out, with Wal-Mart charging $4 or $5 for a case of Coca-Cola, Pepsi, and other top brands, and grocery store competitors matching prices. But for now at least, reports the Atlanta Journal-Constitution, Wal-Mart is keeping prices low to pull in customers seeking deals on their favorite brands of soda and other mainstay products: Wal-Mart’s soda price cuts are part of a broader strategy to reinforce its appeal to budget-minded shoppers, after an effort to emphasize private label items failed to work as well as the chain hoped.
