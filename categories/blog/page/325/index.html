Got a Cold? Have a Beer!

published on May 17, 2024
Drinking beer is good for you and your children, a study by Japan’s oldest brewery says. Hops, one of the three basic ingredients of beer along with water and barley, contains a chemical compound called humulone, which can help the human body fight the virus that causes the common cold in adults, according to a report by the news agency Agence-France Presse. The same virus also can cause bronchitis and pneumonia in children.
