Frank James (Outlaw) - On This Day

Full Name: Alexander Franklin James
Profession: Outlaw

Biography: Frank James was an American outlaw and older brother to the infamous Jesse James. The two were prominent figures in the James-Younger Gang, a notorious group of 19th-century outlaws involved in numerous robberies, heists, and violent incidents during the post-Civil War era.

Interestingly, Frank James distinguished himself by living to old age, a rarity among outlaws of the time. After his younger brother Jesse was killed, Frank surrendered to authorities, facing a series of trials for his past crimes but he was never convicted.

In his later years, Frank James led an oddly conventional life for a former outlaw. He held various jobs, including a shoe salesman and a theater guard.

Born: January 10, 1843
Birthplace: Clay County, Missouri, USA
Star Sign: Capricorn

Died: February 18, 1915 (aged 72)
