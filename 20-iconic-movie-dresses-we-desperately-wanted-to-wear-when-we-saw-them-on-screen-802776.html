20 Iconic Movie Dresses We Desperately Wanted to Wear When We Saw Them on Screen

The cold shoulder red dress from Pretty Woman, the backless green dress from Atonement, and that famous little black dress from Breakfast at Tiffany’s are just a few dazzling outfits that made the movies we love even more beautiful. Looking at our favorite actresses sparkling in stunning gowns is just one more reason why we like to re-watch movies, and the more beautiful those dresses are, the longer they stay in our memories.

We at Bright Side selected 20 of the most iconic dresses we’ve seen in movies, and we bet you wanted to try on at least a couple of them, just like we did.
