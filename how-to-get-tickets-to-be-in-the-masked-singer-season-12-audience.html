The Masked Singer Season 11 just wrapped up last week, which means it’s time to start thinking about Season 12. Fans can already request tickets to be in the audience for tapings, which start in June. The best part? It’s free!

How to Get Tickets for The Masked Singer Season 12

The Masked Singer is returning to FOX for Season 12, expected to premiere this fall. It not yet known if Nicole Scherzinger will be back on the panel, or if Rita Ora will continue to fill in for her. Either way, filming begins very soon.

Tickets are now available for filming dates between June 6 and June 11, with more to come. Fans can request their tickets through the On Camera Audiences website, which lists dates through June 26. You can submit your information to be notified when tickets for those dates are available.

The page for The Masked Singer teases that the next season will feature “BIGGER themes” and “the BIGGEST celebrities and costumes to date.” Each person must submit their own request for a ticket, so be sure to let your friends and family know.

Tapings take place in Los Angeles, and audience members must be at least 12 years old to attend. As of right now, all tapings are scheduled for 4:30 p.m. on their respective days.

Season 11 of the Show Just Wrapped Up

The Masked Singer Season 11 just came to an end last week, as Goldfish was named the winner and revealed to be High School Musical star Vanessa Hudgens. The runner-up was Gumball, who was unmasked as Friday Night Lights star Scott Porter.

Longtime panelist Nicole Scherzinger had to sit the season out, as she was performing on the West End in a production of Sunset Boulevard. Singer Rita Ora — who has appeared on the UK version of the show — filled in for her on the panel.
