Courteney Cox, 58, Looks Decades Younger After She Gives Herself a Makeover

Courteney Cox, 58, proves we’re never too old to follow the latest trends. The actress recently posted an adorable video on her Instagram, where she gives herself a makeover inspired by Gen Z. And her transformation, which was on point, didn’t leave her followers indifferent.

Courteney showed us her creative side.

The Instagram reel shows Courteney scrolling through social media on her phone and looking at Gen Z girls. After being inspired by their style, the star says, “So cute. I wanna do that,” and gets up to give herself a makeover.

The Friends alum fixes her hair in bubble braids, applies shimmery eye shadow, does a cat eye, and then proceeds to change her outfit. She chooses an ab-baring crop top and low-rise baggy jeans and pairs the ensemble with an oversized hoodie.

After her trendy transformation, the star takes phone selfies while holding up the peace sign.

She accompanied the video with the caption: ’’Am I slaying this right?’’ and her post got a lot of attention from fans as well as other celebrities.

For example, fellow actress Lindsay Lohan commented with a series of cheerful emojis, while another friend wrote, ’’Look at those abs!’’

By sharing this adorable reel, Courteney proved that we can always stay young at heart and are never too old to keep up with the most trendy looks.

And we’re sure that the actress gets along perfectly well with her only daughter, born in 2004, who’s part of the Gen Z group, which includes those born from 1997 to 2013.

Her spirit will stay eternally young.

Not so long ago, Cox opened up about aging and admitted that she couldn’t believe that she was turning 60 soon.

The actress noted, “There’s nothing wrong with being 60, I just can’t believe it. Time goes so fast.”
She also acknowledges that time has taught her to be “more grounded.”

And while she’s become more mature with age, Courteney also keeps her youthful spirit and bubbly personality and admits that she feels much younger than her age.

She shared, “I feel like I’m young. I have a lot of friends in their 30s, and I don’t think about it. To me, we’re the same age until I actually study it.”

And by being so comfortable in her own skin, Cox is helping spread plenty of positive vibes, which makes us admire this actress even more.
