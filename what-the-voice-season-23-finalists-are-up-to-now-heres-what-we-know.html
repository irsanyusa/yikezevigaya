What 'The Voice' Season 23 Finalists are Up To Now Heres What We Know

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing The Voice

Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

After drawing to a close last May, The Voice season 23 made its bittersweet goodbye to the finalists. Nonetheless, the journey is no way done for them, as they continue to eagerly pursue their career in music.

We tried to look for updates regarding the lives of the show’s season 23 finalists — Grace West, D. Smooth, Sorelle, NOIVAS, and Gina Miles. If you’re curious to know what they’ve been up to recently, just keep reading. 

Grace West, Team Blake

After The Voice, Grace West didn’t waste any chance and hopped right into the active pursuit of her music career. She attended her first CMA Festival, hinted at a collaboration with Apple music, and shared that she wrote a song with Bryan White and Paige King Johnson. Later, she updated fans that she’s “written some of my favorite songs” using the guitar Blake Shelton gave her. Beyond that, West has also been performing in different shows around Nashville, Tennessee. 

D. Smooth, Team Kelly

Since the end of his stint on season 23, D. Smooth has received overwhelming love and support from his hometown, Birmingham, England. He had been featured by iheartradio and recognized by his high school for the “Our FAVORITE Voice” award. Recently, he was honored by the National Association of Black Journalists for his contribution to Black excellence.

Sorelle, Team Chance

From lip sync challenges to fit checks, singing sisters Sorelle have recently been enjoying creating content on TikTok. Nonetheless, they are not leaving music behind, recording covers of recent pop hits. The trio lately released a covers of “Vampire” by Olivia Rodrigo, “Daylight” by Harry Styles, and “Part of Your World” from The Little Mermaid. 

NOIVAS, Team Blake

After The Voice, NOIVAS happily returned to and celebrated the feat with his wife and two kids. Last July, he shared that he was working with band West Texas Exiles for “new music and videos coming soon.” The following month, he revealed that he’ll be releasing an Americana Country album. 

Gina Miles, Team Niall

Upon winning The Voice season 23, Gina Miles has continued to create music of her own. She’s recently uploaded a snippet of her unreleased single temporarily titled “Someone New.” She also in July expressed appreciation for Sacramento Firefighters for inviting her to sing the National Anthem for their Academy Graduation Ceremony.
