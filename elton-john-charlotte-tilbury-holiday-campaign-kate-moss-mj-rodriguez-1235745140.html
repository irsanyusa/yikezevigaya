Elton John Stars in Dazzling Charlotte Tilbury Holiday Campaign Alongside Kate Moss, MJ Rodrig

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, Variety may receive an affiliate commission.

You know the holidays are coming when Charlotte Tilbury launches her annual holiday campaign, which always features bold-faced names and the brand’s newest products.

This year, the beauty guru’s campaign stars Elton John, and models Kate Moss and Jourdan Dunn, plus Rina Sawayama, Michaela Jaé Rodriguez, as well as her nieces Sofia Schwarzkopf-Tilbury and Bella Broekman-Tilbury to celebrate her favorite time of the year.

“Elton’s always been a huge inspiration to me my entire life,” Tilbury told People Magazine upon the campaign announcement. “He’s a musical genius, a true creative, a renaissance man and a national treasure. He’s loved by all generations.”

“Music is just like makeup. It has the power to change your frequency, to morph your mood and bring joy into people’s lives,” she added. “I always say, give everyone the right makeup and skincare, and they can conquer the world. I want everyone else to feel that way.”

The campaign not only celebrates the holidays, but also marks the brand’s new partnership with Sir Elton John Aids Foundation‘s The Rocket Fund. As the founding beauty partner, Tilbury’s personal contribution to the Rocket Fund will support the Elton John AIDS foundation in helping over 40,000 young people most at risk of HIV and AIDS access life saving services and support.

Timed to the campaign and partnership announcement, the brand has also released two brand new lipsticks co-designed by John. The bestselling Matte Revolution lipstick is now available in two new shades: Rocket Girl, a cool-toned nude pink, and Ready for Lust, a darker deep berry red ($38 each or $72 for the duo). Both hues are made from the brand’s signature formula, infused with lipstick tree extract, vitamin E and a nourishing blend of oils and waxes.

Shop through Charlotte Tilbury’s newest products at Charlottetilbury.com and Sephora.com.
