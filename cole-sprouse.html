Cole Sprouse (Actor) - On This Day

Profession: Actor

Biography: Sprouse rose to fame with his identical twin brother Dylan as child actors. Their first major role came alongside Adam Sandler in Big Daddy (1999), followed by Cole's appearance as Ross Geller's son Ben on Friends (1994-2004).

They shot to stardom with the Disney Channel series The Suite Life of Zack and Cody (2005-8), in which Cole played Cody, and its spin-off, The Suite Life on Deck (2008-11). After a hiatus to attend university, Cole returned to acting as Jughead Jones in the television show Riverdale (2017-present).
