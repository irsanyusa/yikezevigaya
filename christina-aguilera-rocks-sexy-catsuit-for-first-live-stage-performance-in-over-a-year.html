Christina Aguilera wore a sexy black latex catsuit to celebrate the opening of Virgin Hotels Las Vegas this week. This four-day event kicked off to a hot start with the former The Voice coach showing everyone that she literally does not age. Fans on social media are going wild for the five-time Grammy winners bold look.

Christina Aguilera Looks Sexier Than Ever in Black Latex Catsuit

Aguilera’s last show in front of a live audience also happened to be in Las Vegas in March 2020. She returned to Los Angeles when most of the world went into lockdown due to the Covid-19 pandemic. The 40-year-old mom of two reflected on returning to the stage over one year later.

“Last night was an incredible full circle moment — reuniting with my fam on stage and being able to get back to what I love & was born to do…now recharged, reinspired and focused,” she wrote on Instagram. “It was wonderful to see everyone’s beautiful faces in the crowd, feeling the love and joy that can only be communicated through live music & connection through song! It’s a special feeling and I can’t wait to share more music and fun in the future!!
Back at it!!”

Her outfit was a hit with fans who said it was the “the perfect look” and “absolutely amazing” in the comments section on Instagram. Aguilera performed crowd favorite “Lady Marmalade” during the show. In March, she celebrated the 20th anniversary of the song with Pink, Lil Kim, Missy Elliott, and Mya. The song won two MTV Video Music Awards and still goes down as one of the most epic collaborations of all time.

Aguilera rocked a similar catsuit look on her 40th birthday back in December in an Instagram video. She strutted down the hallway in the gorgeous garment to Megan Thee Stallion’s “Body” and rocked a high ponytail. Ahead of her sexy Catwoman-inspired performance this time, Aguilera posted a few pictures in an elevator. She wore her cozy attire and held a cup of coffee. She can even somehow make an elevator look glamorous.

After owning the stage, Aguilera posted a photo relaxing on top of a pool table. She changed into a comfy black outfit and sipped a drink out of a solo cup. The “Fighter” singer stuck her tongue out and threw a peace sign knowing that she absolutely slayed her performance.
