Attending an 80s party: here are 6 style inspirations (for girls)

Big hair

Perms, perms, and more perms. Whether it was big bangs, big curls, or big feathers, 80s hair was just big.

Spandex

ADVERTISEMENT

Lycra changed the world, and the 80s made sure it knew. Everyone was wearing spandex. Men, women, boys, girls, it didn’t matter. Spandex was the material of the future, and everyone was in on the trend.

Ripped knees

When folks weren’t wearing skin-tight leggings, they were wearing jeans. Whether you bought them that way or cut them out yourself, every pair of jeans had the knees torn out. They are called ‘crazy jeans’ today.

Leg warmers

These are pants for your calves only.

ADVERTISEMENT

High waisted jean

Now commonly referred to as mom jeans, these high rise cuties made a statement back in the 80s and still do today.

Neon colours

Why go big and bold if you’re just going to use muted colours? It doesn’t make any sense. That’s why neon colours were everywhere in the 80s. Plus, the neons really popped at the skating rink during the black light skate.

Over the last few years, famous television series and movies set in the 80s have gained popularity in mainstream media and this sensation has seen the revival of major fashion trends of the 80s. The fashions of the 80s are one that often initiates nostalgia for a more simplistic time, and wonder for the new generation who never got a taste of it. These reasons are actually why many individuals throw themed 80s parties and its fashion appeal is on the rise.
