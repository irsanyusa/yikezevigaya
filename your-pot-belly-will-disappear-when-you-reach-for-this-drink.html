Warm water for a protruding belly

The morning ritual of drinking coffee is an integral part of the day for many of us. Although this is not a highly harmful habit for healthy people, it is worth considering an alternative in the form of a cup of warm water. Why can this simple change benefit us?

Why should water be warm and not cold? Cold water causes blood vessels to constrict and slows down digestion, while warm water has the opposite effect. The ideal temperature is around 36 degrees Celsius. If we want to get rid of belly fat, we should drink warm water.

Even a small amount of warm water drunk on an empty stomach has the ability to stimulate metabolism. Additionally, it improves intestinal function and can prevent constipation problems. This simple procedure can therefore support the weight loss process. But it is not everything. Water in the morning is also an excellent way to cleanse the body and get rid of toxins.
