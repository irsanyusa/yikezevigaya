The winner of The Voice season 24, Huntley has fans buzzing about his new record deal. Cryptic posts on the singer’s Instagram stories have signaled that the singer is visiting Republic Records in New York City. For those who may not know, part of The Voice‘s winning prize is a record deal with Republic Records.

Huntley Visits Republic Records in New York City

Huntley captured the hearts of viewers across America with his gravelly voice and contagious charm on Season 24 of The Voice. The singer-songwriter cemented his win with memorable performances week after week, showcasing impressive range and artistry.

Now Huntley is teasing upcoming career moves after his triumphant run on the show. Republic Records is one of the “big three” labels in the industry and is home to superstars like Drake, Ariana Grande, The Weeknd, and Taylor Swift. His partnership with them is set to significantly boost Huntley’s reach. As a rising talent in country pop/rock music, he fits Republic Records’ roster well.

So far, Huntley has dropped some intriguing hints about Republic Records on Instagram. In one post, he shared a photo of himself with two others in a car, writing “Heading to NYC with the team,” where he also tagged the label’s account.

Additionally, the singer posted an Instagram story video singing in the streets of New York City. The posts coincided with his trip to the area shortly after his The Voice victory.

Huntley Earned His Record Deal While on The Show

Huntley has the voice, artistry, and work ethic to thrive with a major label like Republic Records. His incredible journey on The Voice under Team Niall displayed his elite vocal control and range across multiple genres.

Prior to the show, Huntley honed his craft over years of live performances and competing in local talent shows. He exhibited persistence by auditioning for American Idol twice and making it to Hollywood.

Moreover, Huntley’s sheer determination to provide for his family led him to take a break from music and then courageously restart his career. His heartfelt story and family-man appeal endeared him to viewers.

Now, after winning The Voice and amassing a huge fanbase, Huntley has the platform and momentum to become country music’s next big star. His talent and dedication make him a perfect fit for Republic Records’ roster.
