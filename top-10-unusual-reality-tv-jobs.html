Top 10 Unusual Reality TV Jobs

OCCUPATION(S): Itinerant TV-commercial directors

ABOUT THE SHOW: Rhett McLaughlin and Link Neal create local-television commercials: their low-budget high-impact spots — imbued with their deliriously goofball sensibilities (Chuck Testa, anyone?) — have earned them a loyal online following, and more than 90 million video views. In this IFC show, these two best friends travel across the country, finding local businesses in need of their unique talents.

TYPICAL MOMENT(S): Meeting and working with Margaret, the sweet and adorably kooky proprietress of the Holiday Hotel for Cats.
