Top model shows off bikini body

The Nigerian model based in the US has looked good as she flaunted her famous toned svelte frame in the two piece she wore while having fun times on the beach.

In an image she chose a barely there two piece sans makeup with air dripping wet. For another she chose a two piece with bold graphic details complete with a cover up. She also scored another matching set with multi coloured stripes aired with reflective sunnies.

Ojy Okpe is married with two kids, she was scouted at age seventeen (17) after which she entered for the Face of Africa Competition where she emerged a finalist.

She's walked for top designers including Oscar de la Renta, Jean Paul Gaultier, Versace, Moschino, Ralph Lauren and Dolce & Gabbana. She walks  for legendary swimwear brand, Gottex every season at fashion week.
