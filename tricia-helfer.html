Tricia Helfer (Model and Actress)

Profession: Model and Actress

Biography: Tricia Helfer is a Canadian actress and former model, best known for her role as Number Six, a humanoid Cylon, in the reimagined Battlestar Galactica television series (2004-2009). Her captivating performance in the series garnered her significant acclaim and a strong fan following.

Helfer's acting career began after her successful modeling stint, and she soon landed roles in various television series and films. Some notable appearances include guest roles in popular TV shows like Criminal Minds (2005-2020), Burn Notice (2007-2013), and Lucifer (2016-2020).

In addition to her on-screen work, Helfer has lent her voice to several video game characters, most notably as Sarah Kerrigan in the StarCraft II series (2010-2015) and EDI in the Mass Effect series (2010-2012).

Born: April 11, 1974
Birthplace: Donalda, Alberta, Canada
