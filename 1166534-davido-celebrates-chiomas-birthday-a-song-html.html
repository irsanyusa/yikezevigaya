Davido celebrates Chiomas birthday with a new song

- Davido has released a song dedicated to his girlfriend Chioma

- Titled Assurance, Davido sings about how Chioma is the love of his life

- He was also seen declaring his love for her in a club

It’s no longer news that popular Nigerian musician Davido is in a relationship with a lady named Chioma Avril Rowland. Both have taken their relationship ‘public’ a number of times.

Both recently visited Barbados on vacation and they looked very much in love with each other. Chioma turns a year older on April 30, 2018 and to celebrate, Davido released a song dedicated to her.

Titled Assurance, the album art of the song sees Davido and Chioma posing together. Chioma who is a chef is dressed in her uniform and she has Davido’s 30 billion gang chain on.
