How can you tell someone you miss them without the word MISS through text?

Missing someone means that you crave for their presence, touch, feel, or generally their vibe when you are together. It could either be that you desire to spend quality time with a specific person, or you genuinely love them, and you wish they were around at a particular time. In addition, missing someone depicts that you are thinking about them.

Do you miss your loved one? Are you wondering how to tell someone you miss them over text without saying it? Well, look no further, for we have compiled a list of ways to express how much you miss someone without actually saying the three words, I miss you.

What can I text instead of I miss you?

There are so many ways of indirectly saying I miss you to someone. Perhaps, you are shy to say the exact words, or you want to express how much you miss someone. Have a look at these other sentiments that you can use to depict the same meaning.
