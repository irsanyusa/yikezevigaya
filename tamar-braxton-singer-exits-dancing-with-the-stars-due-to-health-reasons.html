Singer exits 'Dancing With The Stars' due to health reasons

The singer took to her Instagram to announce that she is stepping away from the ABC competition series due to poor health.

"With a Heavy heart I regret to inform you that my season of #dwts has to come to an end along with the rest of my tour dates with @musicbykem," posting a picture to Instagram from her hospital bed with her partner Val Chmerkovskiy, the 38-year-old wrote.
