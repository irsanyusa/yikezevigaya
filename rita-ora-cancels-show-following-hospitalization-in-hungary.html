The Masked Singer panelist Rita Ora canceled a planned performance in Hungary over the weekend, as she shared on social media that she had been hospitalized the previous day. However, the singer didn’t give any further details about her condition.

Rita Ora Cancels Show in Hungary After Hospital Stay

Over the weekend, Ora was scheduled to perform at Campus Fesztivál in Debrecen, Hungary, but she announced on Instagram stories on Saturday that she would have to cancel. The reason for the cancelation appears to be related to Ora’s health.

“Please forgive this unexpected turn of events but sadly I will not be able to perform at Campus Fesztivál tonight,” Ora wrote on Instagram. “Having spent the night in hospital in Budapest, I must rest thoroughly and follow doctor’s orders.”

The singer apologized to her fans who were attending the show, adding, “I’m truly thankful for your understanding.” Ora didn’t go into detail about the reason for her hospital stay, and she has yet to update fans about her condition as of this writing.

Ora Recently Performed in Lithuania

Just a couple of days before canceling her performance in Hungary, Ora took the stage in Lithuania at a festival called As Young as Vilnius on Thursday. The singer posted a series of photos on Instagram after the performance, writing, “Lithuania you blew my mind!!”

Ora’s next scheduled performance, according to her website, is on August 16 at Lovestream Festival in Bratislava, Slovakia. She also has performances lined up in Timisoara, Romania, and Manchester, England, later that month.

The singer released her latest album, You & I, just over a year ago. Earlier this year, Ora released a new single called “Ask and You Shall Receive,” along with an accompanying music video that features a cameo from her husband, Taika Waititi.
