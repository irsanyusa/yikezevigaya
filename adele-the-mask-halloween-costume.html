Adele Slays Halloween With Realistic 'The Mask' Costume

November 2, 2016 12:11 AM EDT

If there were a gold prize for Halloween costumes this year, Adele would win hands down. To the amazement of her fans, the “Send My Love” singer posted a series of photos to Instagram of her dressed as Jim Carrey’s The Mask from the 1994 classic.

And she got the costume down to a T, with Carrey’s signature zoot suit, polka-dot tie and a strikingly realistic mask. Despite being totally incognito, discerning fans can make out her signature “Angelo” tattoo on her right hand.
