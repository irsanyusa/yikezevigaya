What Happened on December 8, 1949

Historical Events

  • Chinese Nationalist government moves from Chinese mainland to Formosa

Famous Birthdays

  • Mary Gordon, American author (The Company of Women), born in Far Rockaway, New York
  • Ray Shulman, Scottish progressive rock bassist, songwriter (Gentle Giant), and record producer (The Sugarcubes), born in Portsmouth, England (d. 2023)
  • Robert Sternberg, Proposed the Triarchic Theory of Intelligence
