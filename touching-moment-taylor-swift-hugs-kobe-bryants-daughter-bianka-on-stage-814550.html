Touching Moment: Taylor Swift Hugs Kobe Bryants Daughter Bianka on Stage

Vanessa Bryant and her daughters, Bianka and Natalia, joined over 70,000 enthusiastic fans at a Taylor Swift concert. But only Kobe Bryant’s family got a special moment they will never forget.

Vanessa Bryant, the 41-year-old widow of Los Angeles Lakers legend Kobe Bryant, shared a sweet moment on Instagram. She posted a photo of Taylor Swift giving a big hug to her six-year-old daughter, Bianka, during the concert on stage.

During the heartwarming moment, Bianka, dressed in a sparkling pink and blue coat with a pink skirt, shared a warm hug with the pop singer on stage.

“We love you @taylorswift!” Vanessa said both in her Instagram post and story.

Vanessa also shared a special ’Swiftie’ moment on her Instagram story—a blue denim jacket adorned with a meaningful image on the back.

The picture showcased her late husband, Kobe Bryant, celebrating with Taylor Swift her achievement of setting a new record for the most sold-out shows in 2015.

This isn’t the first time Kobe Bryant’s family went public to honor his life and legacy. Vanessa and their kids came together with Kobe’s handprints to express how much he is loved and missed. “I’m honored to be here today to recognize the man that we all knew and love, my dad, Kobe Bryant,” said Natalia Bryant.
